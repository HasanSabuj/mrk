Welcome to HAtS Daycare

Our newly established Daycare is build and equipped to high standard taking in to account your child’s safety at all times.

All of our rooms have been thoughtfully planned to help your child have a free flow day with everything they need within the room. This enables staff to be in the rooms at all times providing optimum supervision.

HAtS daycare is about providing the best level of child care and education for every child who comes through our door. With a highly qualified team of staffs and a management team.

We believe in working closely with all families offering ongoing support and encouraging continual involvement in the nursery life the child. We understand that it is often difficult for parents to put their child in to nursery which is why we hope our day care center will be perfect setting in which you and your child will feel completely safe also at home . As a child care service provider , we try to provide the best services for you.
